## What is OptionBay?

Welcome to the official documentation for **OptionBay**!

OptionBay is a high-performance WooCommerce extension designed to help store owners offer complex product customizations without bloating their site. From simple text fields and quantity-based pricing to complex image swatches and conditional logic, OptionBay provides a unified, visual interface to build and manage product addons.

Whether you're selling custom-printed t-shirts, personalized gift boxes, or complex technical equipment, OptionBay gives you the tools to collect more data from your customers and increase your Average Order Value (AOV).

## Key Features

**Visual Addon Builder:**

- **Drag-and-Drop Interface:** Build complex forms in minutes using our intuitive React-based builder.
- **Multiple Field Types:** Supports Text, Number, Select, Radio, Checkbox, Image Swatches, Color Swatches, and more.
- **Live Preview:** See exactly how your options will look as you build them.

**Dynamic Pricing Engine:**

- **Flexible Price Types:** Add flat fees, percentage increases, or character-count pricing.
- **Live Total Display:** Shoppers see their total price update in real-time on the product page as they select options.
- **WooCommerce Integration:** Addon prices are automatically added to the cart total and displayed clearly in the order summary.

**Rule-Based Assignments:**

- **Global & Specific:** Assign option groups to your entire store, specific categories, individual tags, or hand-picked products.
- **Conditional Logic:** Show or hide specific fields based on the values of other fields, creating a personalized shopping journey.

**Optimized for Performance:**

- **Smart Code Splitting:** Our modern architecture separates the management interface from the settings area to keep your WordPress admin fast.
- **Native Experience:** Integrated directly into the WooCommerce 'Products' menu and settings tab for a seamless workflow.
