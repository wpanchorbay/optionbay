# Option Groups

**Option Groups** are the building blocks of OptionBay. Instead of creating options for every product individually, you create reusable "groups" that can be assigned to multiple products, categories, or tags.

## Creating an Option Group

To create your first group:
1. Navigate to **Products > Options** in your WordPress dashboard.
2. Click the **Add New** button at the top.
3. Enter a **Title** for your group (e.g., "Gift Wrapping Options" or "Technical Specifications"). This title is for your internal reference.
4. Use the **Addon Builder** to add fields to your group.
5. Configure the **Product Assignments** to decide where these options should appear.
6. Click **Save** to publish the group.

## Managing Your Groups

The main Options page provides an overview of all your created groups.

### Group Overview Table
- **Title:** The internal name of the group.
- **Status:** Whether the group is currently active or a draft.
- **Actions:** 
    - **Edit:** Opens the Addon Builder for this group.
    - **Duplicate:** Create an exact copy of the group (useful for creating variations of complex sets).
    - **Delete:** Permanently remove the group and its assignments.

## Best Practices

- **Keep it Modular:** Instead of one massive group with 50 fields, consider breaking them into smaller, focused groups (e.g., "Basic Personalization" and "Premium Addons").
- **Clear Naming:** Use descriptive titles for your groups so you can easily identify them in the assignment rules later.
- **Draft Mode:** Use the "Draft" status while building complex logic to ensure shoppers don't see unfinished options on your store.
